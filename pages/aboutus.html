About Us

Emmanuel MUSHIMIYIMANA

CEO

FONDAMEM

Fondamem is One of the leading Company which sells diffent qualities of water sports materials (Canoes, Paddles,Jerseys Gloves and other sports equipment and fishing materials.)

Established by Rwandan Fisherman Emmanuel MUSHIMIYIMANA, Who doubles as its managing director.

Fondamem started as a small Company in 2020, but it is growing day by day to become one of the largest Company in Rwanda , Serving everywhere in the country their products with its main retail and wholesale shop, located at Nyarugenge Kigali City, Fondamem is known across the region of great lakes for figh qualit water sports materials, sport clothes, boat equipments and fishing accessories.

Fondamem Imports most of its products from countries such as CHINA, UAE, FRANCE and INDIA.
